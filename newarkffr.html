    Food trucks, booths, and pop-up resturaunts to participate in our festival. If you are a local business, have interesting, creative, good food to provide, come join us!

Galleries & Official Stops

Explore our partners and city monthly. Have an offering? APPLY.

City Without Walls • 12pm - 6pm

    City Without Walls is an urban gallery of emerging art that advances the careers of artists while building the audience for contemporary art.
Express Newark • TBP
    A University Community Collaboratory — supports cross-unit, crosssector, cross-institutional publicly engaged scholarship collaborating with Newark artists, schools and institutions to cultivate new talent.
Newark Print Shop • TBP
    The Newark Print Shop (NPS) is a community fine art printmaking studio in Downtown Newark, NJ. Bringing the fine art of printmaking to Newark since 2012!
Artifacts of Newark / Index Art Center • 11am - 6pm
    Index Art Center is a non-profit contemporary arts organization dedicated to supporting and promoting visual arts in Newark, NJ.
Project for Empty Space / Gateway Project Spaces • 11am - 6pm
    A hub for multidisciplinary creative engagement; a highly competitive and curated residency program, a project-based gallery in collaboration with Project For Empty Space, and rentable artist studio/ work spaces.
The Painters Palette • 10am - 10pm
    At the Painter's Palette, you can let your imagination soar! Offering paint and sip parties, as well as private painting parties for your personal needs.
Burger Walla • 11am - 8pm
    Seeks to be a part of the fabric of the city and the neighborhood we are continuing to build. It is about being a cool spot with fresh and tasty food.
Equal Space • 11am - 6pm
    A unique culture, a positive space; a co-working space for intellectuals, techies & entrepreneurs of NJ.
