Participate at Newark First Fridays

We welcome you and your talents, Apply Now:

Arts / Makers / Hand-Crafted Vendors

    Artists, craftspeople, and makers, welcome to share and sell their work with the community!
Food & Beverage Vendors
    Food trucks, booths, and pop-up resturaunts to participate in our festival. If you are a local business, have interesting, creative, good food to provide, come join us!
Staged Performances
    All Greater Newark Area musicians, DJs, producers (amplified and non-amplified), and other performing artists - we want you to perform at NFFr!
Street Performances / Art Installations
    Street performances and art installations are very welcomed in our series. Don't plan on vending? Please provide us with more information about you.
Non-Profit Information & Services
    Would like to have a table or booth for your non-profit or community organization? Apply here. You may request on that form to have the space fee reduced or waived.
Volunteer
    We will connect volunteers by areas of interest to best aid in bringing this platform to the Newark community.

Best vendors in the area.

Unique, intergenerational placemaking and upskill workshops.

Sponsorship

Interested in being a sponsor and/or supporter of this special event that aims to attract thousands each month? We welcome your inquiry about sponsoring the event. Please feel free to email us at zay@galleryretail.co.
